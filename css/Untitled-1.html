A lot of things have changed since we were founded in 1986, but some things never have: connecting with God and each other through biblical teaching, inspiring art and practical next steps that make a difference in the here and now.

WHO WE ARE

WHO WE ARE

We prioritize simple and clear opportunities to help people take their next steps toward Christ…together. We’re building a thriving volunteer culture that provides next-level kids, students and young adult ministries in addition to opportunities for women, men and families. The fun and adventure of life transformation is happening all across our weekly gatherings, events and groups, one relationship at a time.

WE FOCUS ON THESE THREE THINGS

As a church, we’re very clear on what we must prioritize in order to accomplish our vision and live out our values. So we focus on three things, which you’ll see emphasized across the life of the church.

Weekly Gatherings. We have inspiring weekly gatherings for guests and those who already attend, to worship God, discover biblical truths and apply practical next steps.

Events. We offer a wide variety of events to allow you and your friends to grow in relationship with Jesus and experience being a part of something bigger than yourselves.

Groups. We think life is better when we do it together: in life groups, learning groups and serving groups.
